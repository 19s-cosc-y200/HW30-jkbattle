Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go at it alone or join one join one of
our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Wathcing

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise
several times a week.
